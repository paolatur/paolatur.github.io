Roasted Mediterranean Vegetables with Herby Couscous

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 courgette
  • 1 aubergine
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 red onion
  • 4 salad tomatoes
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 150g couscous
  • Handful of fresh parsley
  • 1 lemon

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Chop the courgettes, aubergine, peppers, tomatoes, onions into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Smash the garlic bulb or simply roast the whole bulb.
  4. Place the vegetables on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, squeeze of half a lemon and sprinkle with oregano.
  5. Roast in the oven for 45 minutes, turning halfway through.
  6. Meanwhile, prepare the couscous according to packet instructions.
  7. Mix the roasted vegetables with the couscous and garnish with fresh parsley and squeeze the other half of lemon.
